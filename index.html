Did you know that the Great Wall of China stretches over 21,000 kilometers, making it the longest man-made structure in the world? Machu Picchu remained hidden from the outside world until its rediscovery in 1911 by Hiram Bingham. Meanwhile, Petra in Jordan is also known as the "Rose City" due to the color of the stone from which it is carved. The Christ the Redeemer statue in Brazil was built in 1931 and weighs over 600 tons!

Ancient Wonders were mostly located around the Mediterranean and Middle East, while the Modern Wonders span across several continents, symbolizing the achievements of different civilizations throughout history. These monuments not only represent architectural brilliance but also serve as cultural and historical treasures for humanity.
